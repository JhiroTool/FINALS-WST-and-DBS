About Us

Victoria's Garden and Event Center is your sanctuary for relaxation, celebration, and connection with nature. Our mission is to provide a luxurious yet tranquil experience for every guest, whether you're here for a family getaway, a romantic escape, or a memorable event.

Our Story

Established in 2021, Victoria's Garden was born from a passion for hospitality and a love for nature. Over the years, we've grown into a premier destination for both leisure and events, offering top-notch amenities, personalized service, and breathtaking scenery.

  • Family-owned and operated
  • Eco-friendly and sustainable practices
  • Dedicated to guest satisfaction
Nature Inspired

Surrounded by lush gardens and riverside views, our resort is a true escape into nature.

Luxury & Comfort

Enjoy elegant rooms, fine dining, and premium amenities designed for your relaxation.

Events & Gatherings

Perfect for weddings, reunions, and corporate events with versatile venues and expert planning.

Our Vision

To be the leading riverside resort and event destination, known for our hospitality, sustainability, and unforgettable guest experiences.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional service, luxurious comfort, and a harmonious connection with nature for every guest and event.
